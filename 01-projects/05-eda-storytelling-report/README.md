# EDA Storytelling Report – Retail Sales Analysis

## Problem Statement

This project analyzes retail sales transactions to identify customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and sales trends. The goal is to transform raw transaction data into business insights that can help decision-makers improve revenue and customer engagement.

---

## Dataset

Retail Sales Dataset

---

## Executive Summary

- Product categories contribute differently to total revenue.
- Monthly sales fluctuate over time, indicating possible seasonal demand.
- Spending patterns vary across age groups.
- High-value transactions contribute significantly to total revenue.
- Customer demographics provide opportunities for targeted marketing.

## Recommendations

- Promote top-performing product categories.
- Design targeted campaigns for high-spending customer segments.
- Prepare inventory for seasonal demand.
- Monitor premium customers and high-value purchases.
- Continue collecting customer behavior data for predictive analytics.
